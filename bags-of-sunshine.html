Bags of Sunshine

Bringing joy, hope, and shine—one bag at a time—to pediatric and elderly patients in San Francisco.

Bags of Sunshine is a volunteer, youth‑run project sponsored by Lux et Cura Foundation. Young people and community volunteers prepare and deliver thoughtfully assembled bags filled with high‑quality, safe items to brighten the days of patients who could use extra encouragement and care.

What is a “Bag of Sunshine”?

Each bag is a curated bundle of joy designed to bring comfort, distraction, and delight to kids and older adults receiving care in San Francisco.

Joy in a bag

Bags of Sunshine are gifted to local pediatric and elderly patients who may be facing long days, uncertainty, or isolation. Every bag is assembled by youth and volunteers who want recipients to feel seen, supported, and cared for.

As the project grows, we plan to host fundraising events, run supply drives, and deepen partnerships with hospitals, clinics, and care facilities across the city.

What goes inside

Each bag is filled with safe, age‑appropriate, and engaging items, such as:

  • Lego sets
  • Reading & coloring books
  • Toys & crafts
  • Stuffed animals
  • Journals & notebooks
  • Coloring kits & art kits
  • Crayons & markers
  • Origami paper & mini puzzles
  • Small card games & stickers
  • Stress balls & sensory toys
  • Soft toys
  • Lip balm & wipes

Items may vary by age group and setting, but each bag is built with care, safety, and dignity in mind.

Youth‑Led

Led by youth, supported by community

Bags of Sunshine is designed and run by young volunteers who want to make a tangible difference in their city. Adult mentors provide guidance, but youth voices shape the ideas, outreach, and the contents of each bag.

Through this project, young people learn leadership, empathy, and project‑planning skills while building a culture of kindness and service.

A project of Lux et Cura

The Bags of Sunshine Project is one of several emerging initiatives sponsored by Lux et Cura Foundation to support youth leadership and community‑centered service.

As the foundation grows, Bags of Sunshine will sit alongside other projects that help neighbors care for one another in practical, visible ways.

Volunteer with Bags of Sunshine
