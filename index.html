Lux et Cura Foundation

A volunteer‑driven nonprofit in San Francisco bringing light and care to our communities.

Lux et Cura Foundation is a San Francisco–based nonprofit dedicated to empowering community members and youth through volunteer‑driven service, education, and community initiatives that promote health, happiness, and social responsibility. The Foundation fosters a sense of community by supporting direct volunteer projects, youth leadership development, and collaborative efforts that encourage individuals to be active participants in creating positive and lasting social change.

About Lux et Cura

Grounded in San Francisco, Lux et Cura Foundation channels local energy, skills, and generosity into practical service for neighbors and youth.

What we do

We design and support hands‑on projects that make it easier for community members and youth to show up for each other—through service events, mentoring, and small acts of care.

Our work is intentionally local, collaborative, and flexible so that volunteers can contribute in ways that match their time, interests, and abilities.

How we work

  • Partner with schools, community groups, and local organizations.
  • Center youth voice and leadership in project planning.
  • Keep overhead lean and projects practical, visible, and community‑driven.

Programs & Initiatives

Early projects focus on tangible, high‑touch efforts that build connection and care.

Bags of Sunshine

Care packages assembled and delivered by youth and volunteers to neighbors experiencing illness, isolation, or transition—sharing practical support and a reminder that they are not alone.

Learn more about Bags of Sunshine →

Youth Service & Leadership

Support for youth‑led service projects, from idea to implementation, helping young people learn to plan, organize, and reflect on meaningful community work.

Community Partnerships

Collaborations with local schools, clinics, and community groups to co‑design projects that respond to real needs in San Francisco’s neighborhoods.

Early Impact

As a young foundation, we are starting small and building momentum project by project.

Year 1–2

Focused on a small set of high‑quality pilot projects and partnerships.

Volunteer‑driven

All programs planned and delivered by volunteers, with no paid staff in the early phase.

Story‑first

We track stories, learning, and feedback as carefully as numbers to guide future growth.
